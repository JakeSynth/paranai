An in-depth fantasy setting for Dungeons & Dragons including expansive lore, locations, quest prompts, and more...

Status: Temporary Hiatus

Beset in the sea as a jewel set in an azure crown; the island of Paranai and it's close neighbours jut from the waves as the sole land for weeks sailing in any direction. Titanous beasts beneath the "Outer-Blue" stymie any travellers who dare visit, or attempt to leave, and awakened magics in the very soil are beginning to take their toll on those living there. The continents of Meilur Aaeth at its far east, The Ilsa distant west, and Bhy'allor further still sets the tides bearing curious flotsam to grace the shores of the islands.

The biomes highlighted on the map outline the borders of Difficult Terrain, where movement between points could increase drastically. A walking party would take a day's travel between the points dotted around Paranai normally; It is your choice how this is interpreted, from 8-12 hours to 24 hours. However, when in marked terrains below movement becomes awfully encumbered, and as such moving novel distances could take twice to quadruple the time it would usually take. (This is except for trade routes and ports which are included here for clarity.)
